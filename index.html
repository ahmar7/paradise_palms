Introduction to BTC Ordinal NFTs

Web3 NFTs (on Ethereum and other blockchains) are dependent on token standards such as ERC 721 and have off-chain data. This allows NFTs to be altered by changing their metadata. Thus, some NFTs are occasionally “upgraded.” In contrast, Bitcoin Ordinals (or BTC NFTs) are NFTs that are minted directly onto the Bitcoin blockchain itself, which means all their data and content are permanent and immutable.
